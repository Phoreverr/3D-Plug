About Us

Who We Are

We work with you to make your vision a reality

3D Plug is a pioneering company dedicated to unlocking the full potential of additive manufacturing in the 3D printing industry. Our journey began with a clear vision: to tackle the challenges that have historically limited the adoption of 3D printing and to reshape the manufacturing landscape for businesses across various sectors in Africa.

Our commitments

We are committed to reducing dependence on global supply chains by offering secure and reliable local solutions. One of our core offerings is the production of laboratory consumables and disposables, with a particular focus on medical and pharmaceutical applications.

Our focus

Our team of passionate experts, combined with advanced technology, allows us to make 3D printing more accessible, efficient, and cost-effective than ever. Our solutions empower businesses by equipping them with the necessary tools and capabilities to improve production processes, lower costs, and encourage sustainable practices

OUR MISSION

Our mission is clear and ambitious: to deliver customized, sustainable, and high-quality 3D printing models and prototypes. We empower businesses to optimize their processes, reduce production costs, enhance overall efficiency, and minimize their environmental impact.

To utilize additive manufacturing to produce locally made, high-quality, and cost-effective laboratory consumables and disposables, reducing Africa's dependency on imports. We strive to enhance healthcare and research capabilities across the continent while promoting sustainable practices.

Where we stand

3D Plug stands at the intersection of innovation and accessibility. As both a manufacturer and supplier, we enable institutions to access world-class laboratory solutions that are affordable, adaptable, and locally sourced. We are committed to reshaping Africa’s manufacturing potential, one product at a time.
