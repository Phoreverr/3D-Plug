Who We Are

We work with you to make your vision a reality

3D Plug is a pioneering company dedicated to unlocking the full potential of additive manufacturing in the 3D printing industry. Our journey began with a clear vision: to tackle the challenges that have historically limited the adoption of 3D printing and to reshape the manufacturing landscape for businesses across various sectors in Africa.

Our commitments

We are committed to reducing dependence on global supply chains by offering secure and reliable local solutions. One of our core offerings is the production of laboratory consumables and disposables, with a particular focus on medical and pharmaceutical applications.
