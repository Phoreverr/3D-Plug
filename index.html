FAQs

Everything you need to know about us

What types of projects can you design?

We can create custom 3D designs for prototypes, functional parts, product models, architectural elements, art pieces, and more, based on your exact requirements.

Do I need to have a design ready before working with you?

Yes, we can. Our Not at all, our team can start from your idea, sketch, or concept and develop a professional 3D model for printing.

What materials do you use for 3D printing?

We offer a variety of materials, including PLA, ABS, PETG, resin, and specialty filaments, chosen to match your project’s purpose and durability needs.

How long does it take to complete a 3D printing project?

Project timelines vary depending on size, complexity, and finishing requirements, but most orders are completed within a few days to a week.

Can you help me improve an existing 3D design?

Yes, through our consultation services, we can review, optimize, and modify your existing files for better strength, aesthetics, or print efficiency.

Do you offer post-print maintenance or repairs?

Absolutely, we can smooth, repair, reprint, or upgrade parts to ensure they stay functional and in top condition over time.
